## 2. VOICE & TONE ANALYSIS

### Writing Style Characteristics:
- **Direct and conversational** - "Hey folks," "My friends"
- **Numbered breakdowns** - Bullet-heavy for clarity
- **Specific over vague** - "$500M" not "very wealthy"
- **Storytelling through data** - Numbers tell the story
- **Self-aware** - Acknowledges privilege, shares failures
- **Action-oriented** - Always pushing toward doing something

### Sentence Structure:
- Short, punchy sentences mixed with longer explanatory ones
- Heavy use of dashes for tangential thoughts
- Questions to engage readers
- "And by the way..." for asides

### Key Phrases:
- "Here's the thing..."
- "Look, by the way..."
- "What's crazy is..."
- "And so..."
- "The cool thing about..."
